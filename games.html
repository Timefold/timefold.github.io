Tokyo Tiers

In the game you start at the bottom of society, in a dark and dangerous slum. Your goal? Climb up as fast and as far as possible to the futuristic upper city, where wealth and technology rule. Use parkour and improvisation to climb higher. with various city levels, from industrial zones to the shiny skyscrapers of the elite.

The city is your playground, but the way up is dangerous. How far will you get?
